# AkoweAI

**Cooperative savings management, right inside WhatsApp.**

---

## The Problem

Millions of Nigerians participate in cooperative savings groups — locally called _ajo_ or _esusu_ — where members pool money together on a regular schedule. Today, most of these groups are managed with paper records, WhatsApp text threads, and manual bank transfers. There is no transparency, no automated tracking, and no way to know at a glance who has paid and who hasn't.

The obvious solution would be to build an app. The problem is getting everyone to use it. Members are already on WhatsApp. Moving them to a new platform means downloads, signups, and habits they may never form.

## The Solution

AkoweAI manages your cooperative entirely through WhatsApp. Members pay, check their balance, and view their contribution history without ever leaving the app they already use every day. Executive committee members (exco) get additional admin controls through the same WhatsApp interface, plus a dedicated web dashboard for a full overview.

No app to install. No account to create. Just WhatsApp.

---

## Features

**For Members (via WhatsApp)**

- Register and join a cooperative using a simple invite code
- Make contributions securely via a payment link sent directly in chat
- Check current balance and full contribution history on demand
- Receive automated reminders as due dates approach
- Multi-cooperative support — switch between groups in the same chat

**For Exco / Admins (via WhatsApp + Web Dashboard)**

- View real-time cooperative status and pool balance
- Look up individual member payment history
- Record and notify members of pool withdrawals
- Generate and manage member invite codes
- Send broadcast messages to all members
- AI-powered contribution summaries and member risk scoring
- Conversational dashboard chatbot for quick queries
